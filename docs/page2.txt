Adding a person to your notebook:
To add someone, simply click the "Add" button and fill in the entries.
When you're done, you can just click the "Add" button that appears on the
window.

Deleting a person from your phonebook:
To delete someone, just select in your phonebook and click "Delete".

Editing data:
If you accidentally made a typo when adding someone, or want to change their
data, select them and click "Edit".
When you're done filling the entries with updated data, just hit "Edit".

Resetting:
If you reset, you delete all of your phonebook data in the program and you're no
longer signed in.
However, if you have exported your data, you can simply re-import.
