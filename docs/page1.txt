Welcome to the Phonebook2 docs!
The docs will present you information on how to use this simple and lightweight
program.

Phonebook 2 is a simple and lightweight program that can help you keep
track of your phone numbers. It allows you to add and delete people
in the phonebook, as well as edit them. It can also export your
phonebook to a readable text file, and it can import the data from it
back into the program.

To navigate, click "Next" and "Previous".
